Missions

Our Programs

Flight Duration: 20Days [Lunar Vacation]
The Lunar Vacation is an extraordinary orbital tourism experience that takes travelers on a journey beyond Earth to explore the Moon. Participants will embark on a multi-day mission aboard a specially designed spacecraft that will transport them to lunar orbit. Once in orbit, they will witness breathtaking views of the lunar surface and experience the awe-inspiring sight of Earth rising above the lunar horizon.
Flight Duration: 6 Days [Mars Flyby]
The Mars Flyby is a once-in-a-lifetime orbital tourism adventure that offers travelers the chance to witness the Red Planet up close and personal. This mission involves a journey around Mars, providing a close flyby of the planet without landing on its surface. Participants will travel aboard a state-of-the-art spacecraft equipped with advanced life support systems and comfortable accommodations for an extended duration spaceflight.
Flight Duration: 10 Days [Space Station Getaway]
The Space Station Getaway is a remarkable orbital tourism package that allows individuals to visit and temporarily stay aboard a space station, experiencing life as an astronaut in microgravity. Participants will embark on a space journey to the International Space Station (ISS) or a dedicated commercial space station, where they will live and work alongside professional astronauts.
