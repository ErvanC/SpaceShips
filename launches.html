What's Hot

Launch Date - 7 May 2023 [The Stardust Expedition]
The Stardust Expedition is a groundbreaking space mission aimed at collecting and studying extraterrestrial dust and particles from a distant comet. The primary objective of this mission is to gain valuable insights into the origins and composition of comets, as well as their potential role in the formation of our solar system.
Launch Date - 23 May 2023 [Titan Explorer]
The Titan Explorer mission aims to explore Saturn's largest moon, Titan, and unravel its intriguing mysteries. Titan is known for its unique atmosphere and hydrocarbon-rich environment, making it a prime target for scientific investigation. The mission will deploy a specialized spacecraft equipped with advanced instruments to study Titan's surface, atmosphere, and potential for supporting life.
Launch Date - 11 June 2023 [Exoplanet Quest]
The Exoplanet Quest mission is dedicated to discovering and characterizing exoplanets in our galaxy, expanding our knowledge of planetary systems beyond our own. With the advancement of astronomical technology, this mission aims to identify and study exoplanets located within a range of habitable zones, where conditions may be suitable for the existence of liquid water and potentially life as we know it.
